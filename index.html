INFORMATION

Dianne Weinthal is an artist based in Los Angeles, California. She asks questions about how we interact with image as a form of representation. Her practice is to create stillness & presence through sensory experience.

She received her Masters in Library and Information Science from UCLA & Bachelors in Art History from Stanford University. Her lava lamp is purple.
